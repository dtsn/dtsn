--- meta: _edit_lock: "1271605117" _edit_last: "2" title: Script.aculo.us Transitions published: false tags: - JavaScript - Scriptaculous layout: post type: post status: draft --- I’ve been digging through the Script.aculo.us code a lot recently and have stumbled upon a number of very effective transitions which aren’t used widely. There are 9 effects in total comprising of: Transition
  • Transition
  • Linear
  • Reverse
  • Flicker
  • Wobble
  • Pulse
  • Spring
  • None
  • Full
You can see a full demo of all the the effects on the Script.aculo.us Wiki. Trouble Implementing? All of Script.aculo.us’s effects will take a transition in the options array. For example we could apply a spring transition to our scroll to effect.
