--- title: Script.aculo.us Transitions layout: post ---

I’ve been digging through the Script.aculo.us code a lot recently and have stumbled upon a number of very effective transitions which aren’t used widely. There are 9 effects in total comprising of:

  • Transition
  • Linear
  • Reverse
  • Flicker
  • Wobble
  • Pulse
  • Spring
  • None
  • Full

You can see a full demo of all the the effects on the Script.aculo.us Wiki.

All of Script.aculo.us’s effects will take a transition in the options array. For example we could apply a spring transition to our scroll to effect.
